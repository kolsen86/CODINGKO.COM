Grow Your Brains With These Tutorials and other Learning Resources

One of the most challenging aspects of deciding to teach yourself programming is knowing where to look. We live in an age where all the information you could ever need is right at your fingertips. That very same advantage can also turn into a disaster if you don't know where to look. The internet is full of thousands of tutorials and courses to help you learn to program. The problem is that half of them are either outdated or half way finished. Well while I have been self-teaching myself programming, I have kept note of the truly exceptional tutorials and courses available for free (or of little cost) online. While the authors of most of these tutorials could have charged for thier content, they chose not too. In my eyes that makes them fantastic individuals. So please check out thier tutorials and courses and appreciate all the hard work they put in so that you could try to better your life.

Then they improved upon those tools and gave us things like GitHub, CSS Gradient Generators, Color Pickers, and Integrated Development Enviroments (IDEs). Us modern day Homosapiens have only improved on these things our ancestors created all those years ago and created many, many more. These tools make life much easier on programmers and give more control over the final product.
